Categorical Stability across Universal Dependencies Treebanks

The Weighted Average Stability (WAS) score measures how consistently each POS category is distributed across its neighbourhood in feature space. A score near 1 means every category has a tight, homogeneous neighbourhood; lower scores indicate more cross-categorical overlap. Click any treebank to explore its stability and intruder matrices, and to inspect the bridge words that link categories.
